Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a classic Italian dish that combines the simplicity of garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes with the comforting texture of spaghetti. This recipe is a testament to the power of a few simple ingredients to create a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (400 grams)
  • Extra-virgin olive oil (1/2 cup)
  • Garlic cloves (4-6, minced)
  • Red pepper flakes (1-2 teaspoons, optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Parsley (for garnish, optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions, making sure it's al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water before draining.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) and saut√© until the garlic is fragrant but not browned.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the garlic and oil. Toss well to combine, adding a little of the reserved pasta cooking water if needed to create a smooth and creamy sauce.
  4. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade Spaghetti Aglio e Olio!
